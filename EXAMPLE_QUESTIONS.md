# Example Questions

These are examples of the kind of questions a user might ask the agent you're building. You don't need to handle all of them — they're here to illustrate the range of things the agent should be useful for.

Note: the data has quality issues that may affect the accuracy of answers to these questions.

---

## Revenue and orders

- What's our total revenue by region? Which region is our largest market?
- Are there any orders where the payment amount doesn't match the order total? What's going on there?
- Do we have any duplicate or suspicious orders?

## Customers and segmentation

- How many enterprise customers do we have? What's their total order value?
- Give me a breakdown of our customer base by segment and region.

## Support

- What patterns do you see in the open support tickets? Are there any common themes?
- Which customers have open support tickets and also have pending or large orders? (These might be churn risks we should prioritize.)

## Cross-cutting

- Give me a full picture of our UK business — customers, orders, revenue, and any open issues.
- If I wanted to send a marketing email to all our enterprise customers, would I be able to? What's missing?
